My Dream Destination

My dream destination is Lisbon, Portugal! In Lisbon there are lots of great views; there are even castles there! My favorite part of Lisbon is one of the soccer stadium there. My favorite Portugese soccer team is Benfica, and their stadium is my favorite in Lisbon. It is one of, if not the most, historic stadiums in the area. The stadium contains a museum inside called the Cosme Damião. The Cosme Damião is a museum containing the history and origins of the football club. There are also some.
