My Dream Destination - Lisbon

My dream destination is Lisbon, Portugal! In Lisbon there are lots of great views; there are even castles there!

Soccer/Fútbol

My favorite part of Lisbon is one of the soccer stadium there. My favorite Portugese soccer team is Benfica, and their stadium is my favorite in Lisbon. It is called the Estádio da Luz, or Estádio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica. of It is one of, if not the most, historic stadiums in the area.

The Museum Inside of The Estádio da Luz

The stadium contains a museum inside called the Cosme Damião. The Cosme Damião is a museum containing the history and origins of the football club.

Castles and Fortresses

There are also some cool castles and fortresses that you can seee in Lisbon. One of the castles there is called the Castelo de São Jorge. One of the fortresses there is called the Torre de Belém. At the time of making this website, the Torre de Belém is temporarily closed.
